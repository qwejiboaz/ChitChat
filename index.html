How to Play ChitChat

Goal

  • Build the highest hand using cards of the same suit.
  • Number cards = face value. J/Q/K = 10. Ace = 11.
  • Best hand is 31 — Ace + two 10-value cards in one suit.

Your Turn

  • Draw from the Deck or Discard pile.
  • Tap a card in your hand to select it, then tap Discard.
  • You always hold exactly 3 cards.

Ending a Round

  • Knock — ends your turn; everyone else gets one final turn.
  • 31 — instant win; everyone else loses a chip.
  • Highest score wins. All others lose a chip. Ties = all win.
  • Last 2 players tied = replay the hand.

Chips & Elimination

  • Start with 3 chips. Lose all 3 = On Your Honor (one more round).
  • Lose while On Your Honor = eliminated.
  • Last player standing wins!

Multiplayer

  • Host creates a game and shares the 4-digit code via text.
  • Everyone joins on their own device — no passing needed.
  • 1–6 players: 1 deck. 7+ players: 2 decks.
