How to Play ChitChat

Goal

  • Build the highest scoring hand using cards of the same suit.
  • Number cards = face value. Jack, Queen, King = 10. Ace = 11.
  • The best possible hand is 31 — Ace + two 10-value cards of the same suit.

Your Turn

  • Draw a card from the Deck or the top of the Discard pile.
  • Then discard one card from your hand (tap to select, then tap Discard).
  • You always hold exactly 3 cards.

Ending a Round

  • Knock — tap Knock instead of drawing. Everyone else gets one final turn, then hands are revealed.
  • 31 — if you draw to reach exactly 31, the round ends immediately. Everyone else loses a chip.
  • The player(s) with the highest score win the round. All others lose a chip.
  • Ties are all winners — no chip lost. If only 2 players remain and tie, play another hand.

Chips & Elimination

  • Each player starts with 3 chips.
  • Lose all 3 chips and you go On Your Honor — one final round with no chips.
  • Lose while On Your Honor and you're eliminated.
  • Last player standing wins!

Decks

  • 1–6 players: one deck (52 cards).
  • 7+ players: two decks (104 cards).
